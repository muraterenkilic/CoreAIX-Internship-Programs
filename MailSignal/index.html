FAQ

Questions people usually ask.

MailSignal AI is designed to make email alerts smarter without making your inbox harder to manage.

How is this different from normal email filters?

Traditional filters depend on exact words or senders. MailSignal AI understands the meaning of an email and compares it to your priorities.

Can I choose what counts as important?

Yes. You can create prompts like “notify me about internship interviews,” “alert me about bills,” or “send alerts for security warnings.”

Does it notify me about every email?

No. The goal is the opposite. MailSignal AI only sends alerts when an email matches one of your important topics.

Can it summarize important emails?

Yes. Each alert can include a short summary explaining what the email is about and why it was marked as important.

Who is this useful for?

It is useful for students, job seekers, freelancers, business owners, and anyone who receives too many emails but cannot afford to miss important ones.
